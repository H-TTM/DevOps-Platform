DevOps is a combination of practices and principles affecting IT delivery, culture, tooling and processes which enable organisations to improve their customer satisfaction through improved adaptability and the ability to compete more effectively through increased efficiency.


Yet, trying to understand the different aspects and topics of DevOps can be extremely difficult as it is often weighed down with technical jargon which requires years of experience to understand.


DevOps Platform aims to provide a Platform of Understanding for any level of technical expertise by providing a comprehensive, non-technical perspective to give a clear and simple view of what is included in DevOps and how it enables IT delivery to achieve:


Increased Deployment frequency

Decreased time to restore service

Decreased change failure rate

Decreased lead time for changes
