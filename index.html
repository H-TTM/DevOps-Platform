Demystifying DevOps

By Creating a platform of understanding

DevOps Platform

DevOps is a combination of practices and principles effecting IT delivery, culture, tooling and processes, which enable organisations to improve thier customer satisfaction through improved adaptability and compete more effectively through increased efficiency


Yet trying to understand the different aspects and topics of DevOps can be extremely difficult as it is often wieghed down with technical jargon which requires years of experience to understand.


DevOps Platform aims to provide a Platform of Understanding for any level of technical expertise by using a non-technical perspective to provide a clear and simple view of what is included in DevOps and why enables IT delivery to achieve:


Increased Deployment frequency

Decreased time to restore service

Decreased change failure rate

Decreased lead time for changes
