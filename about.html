DevOps Platform

What's it all about?

Hi, my name is Duncan Moreland, I created DevOps Platform to try and make DevOps more understandable and relateable.

I am not technical, far from it, however I have worked in IT for 5 years for IBM, undertaking a range of roles which require me to understand highly technical methods, principles, approaches etc. in order to lead projects and make them run successfully. I also have a key role in IBM's UK&I DevOps Practice and work closely with IBM's European DevOps Senior Leadership.

DevOps is a personal interest of mine, it's a rising movement in IT which has the potential to change how all IT organisations operate significantly. Yet trying to understand DevOps (and anything related to IT) can be extremeley difficult. This is because most IT related Blogs and websites use large amounts of technical jargon that make it almost impossible to understand without multiple degrees and/or significant experience working directly in the IT industry.

The aim of DevOps Platform is to use my personal experience and other related onlines sources to create Blogs on DevOps which are informative and understandable for anyone that has an interest in DevOps.

If you have any questions feel free reach out to me via LinkedIn here.
